Yeston and Kopit’s Phantom of the Opera (TV Miniseries)

The Phantom (Erik)…..Charles Dance
Christine…..Teri Polo
Raoul…..Adam Storke (Phillipe)

Adapted from Yeston and Kopit’s musical, this made-for-TV miniseries from 1990 had the unusual distinction of filming almost all of its scenes at the actual opera house in Paris where the story is supposed to occur. It is also unusual in that it changes Raoul’s name to Phillipe, which was the name of his older brother in the book. Erik himself is gentler than usually portrayed and has not lived his life in complete solitude; he directed the opera’s affairs through the manager Gerard Carriere (Burt Lancaster), whom he also considered his friend.
